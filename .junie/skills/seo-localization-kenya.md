# Skill: Local SEO & Content Localization (Kenya)

## Purpose
Ensure SEO/content features reflect Kenyan search behavior and multilingual needs.

## Core Heuristics
1. Include locality context (estate, town, county) in keyword strategy.
2. Support English/Swahili code-switching naturally.
3. Prioritize intent-driven content (near me, price, availability, urgent service).
4. Enforce on-page SEO correctness (meta, headings, schema, internal links).
5. Balance AI generation speed with editorial trust and quality checks.

## Outputs
- Keyword clusters with intent labels
- Content briefs and bilingual SEO drafts
- Internal-linking plans (pillar-cluster)
